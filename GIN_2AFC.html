Gaps-in-Noise Test (touch friendly)

Instructions

In each trial, you will hear two intervals of white noise. One of the intervals will contain a brief silent gap. Your task is to identify which interval (first or second) contained the gap. If you are unsure, you can select "Unsure".

Press "Play Trial" to begin. After listening, select your answer. The gap duration will adjust based on your responses to find your threshold.

Try to minimize distractions and use headphones for the best experience. Test using one ear to replicate the conditions of the official GIN test.
